our mission

at icepops we're devoted to making homeade flavors and bringing families together. it's what drives us to create an unforgetable ice cream and popscicle experience. we take pride in our locally sourced ingredients, which comes from farms right here in atlanta.

as a business we believe in growing a business with a commuity of people that embodies artful attention to detail and great customer experience. using wholesome ingredients and handmaking our ice creams and pops is what makes icepops so special to family, friends and the great community that brings us together.
